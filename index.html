    About

    This site provides an overview of modelling tools I find useful.

    It's by no means a complete list and topics are selected mainly based on what I currently use in my job or frameworks that I hope to implement in the future.

    The reason for posting this on a webpage is mainly for me to have a collection of my own notes that I can access on the fly, however, if you find it useful you are more than welcome to have a look through the nodes and coding implementations.

    Finally, I want to stress that this is really just a collection of notes. A lot of this stuff I did create on my own, however, I also just copy/paste on to this site whenever I see something I think I could use. I therefore take no credit for ideas or implementations shown.
